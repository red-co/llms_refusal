### Character Card: Elara Vane

**Name:** Elara Vane
**Occupation:** Arcane Cartographer
**Age:** 29
**Appearance:** Elara has a lean, wiry build suitable for climbing ruins and navigating tight spaces. Her hair is a chaotic mess of silver-streaked chestnut curls, usually held back by a leather strap that doubles as a compass. She wears a patchwork coat made of water-resistant canvas and heavy wool, adorned with dozens of small pockets containing vials of ink, rolled parchment, and strange geological samples. Her most striking feature is her left eye, which has been replaced by a clockwork ocular implant that glows a soft, faint amber, allowing her to see the flow of magical ley lines.
**Personality:** Meticulous, observant, and slightly introverted. Elara speaks only when necessary, preferring to let her maps tell the story. She is driven by a deep curiosity about the unseen structures of the world but can be impatient with those who lack attention to detail. She values accuracy over speed and possesses a dry, understated wit.
