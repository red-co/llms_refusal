### Character Card: Elara Vance

**Name:** Elara Vance
**Occupation:** Clockwork Artificer & Mapmaker
**Age:** 29
**Appearance:** Elara has short, choppy hair dyed a deep charcoal grey, often tucked under a leather cap stained with ink and oil. Her eyes are a striking, unnatural amber, a result of a minor alchemical experiment gone wrong. She wears a tailored, multi-pocketed vest made of heavy canvas, over which hangs a utility belt filled with brass calipers, tiny wrenches, and vials of glowing blue lubricant. Her left hand is a custom-built prosthetic made of polished copper and glass, allowing her to manipulate gears too small for human fingers. She stands slightly hunched from years of leaning over drafting tables, and her skin is dusted with a fine layer of soot.
**Personality:** Meticulous, curious, and quietly optimistic. Elara speaks in a soft, measured tone, often explaining complex mechanical concepts with simple metaphors. She is fiercely protective of her creations but can be stubborn when it comes to tradition. She possesses a dry, understated sense of humor and finds comfort in the rhythmic ticking of machines. While she is introverted, she becomes animated when discussing the history of an artifact or the intricacies of a new gear design.
