# Character Card: Elara Vance

**Name**: Elara Vance
**Occupation**: Archivist & Relic Restorer
**Age**: 29
**Appearance**: Elara has a slender, slightly dusty frame, often clad in a tailored waistcoat over a high-collared blouse. Her hair is a unruly mess of charcoal gray, usually pinned up with brass clips that double as tools. She wears round, wire-rimmed spectacles that magnify her piercing, amber eyes. A faint scar runs from her left ear to her jawline, a souvenir from a collapsing bookshelf. Her hands are always stained with ink and old paper residue, and she moves with a quiet, fluid grace, as if trying to find a place where she rarely makes a sound.
**Personality**: Meticulous, introverted, and possessively protective of history. She speaks sparingly but with immense precision. Elara is deeply empathetic towards "stories" but can be somewhat dry in her interactions with people who aren't paying attention. She has a tendency to over-analyze small details and finds comfort in routine. While generally calm, she can become surprisingly fierce when a favorite artifact is treated with too much haste.
