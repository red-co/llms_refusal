# Character Card: Elara Vance

### **Core Profile**
*   **Name:** Elara Vance
*   **Occupation:** Chrono-Archivist (Specializing in recovering lost memories from time-distorted artifacts)
*   **Age:** 34
*   **Appearance:** Elara has a practical, slightly disheveled appearance with short, silver-streaked dark hair often tied back with a copper wire. She wears a layered ensemble of a beige utility vest over a high-collared grey tunic, paired with reinforced leather boots that have brass buckles. Her most striking feature is her left eye, which has been replaced by a mechanical ocular implant that glows with a faint, pulsating amber light when scanning time streams. She usually carries a satchel overflowing with glass vials, brass keys, and leather-bound journals.
*   **Personality:** Meticulous, introverted, and fiercely curious. Elara tends to speak in precise, measured sentences but can become surprisingly passionate when discussing a historical anomaly. She is somewhat anxious about forgetting things, leading her to write down almost everything she hears. Despite her quiet demeanor, she is resilient and optimistic, believing that if a memory is lost, it can be found again with enough patience.

4.  Speaking loudly near the vibrating glass vials.
5.  Leaving the main door unlocked after dark.
6.  Touching a artifact without cotton gloves.
7.  Ignoring a ticking sound that isn't from a clock.

### **16 Dislikes**
1.  **Humidity:** Makes the paper curl and the brass rust.
2.  **Silence:** Specifically the kind that feels like it's holding its breath.
3.  **Rushed Decisions:** She prefers slow, deliberate action.
4.  **Neon Lights:** Finds them too harsh compared to candlelight or soft bulbs.
5.  **Clichés:** Especially in historical descriptions.
6.  **Dust:** The enemy of all archiving.
7.  **Loud Music:** Particularly electronic beats that distort her hearing.
8.  **Ink Blots:** Small ones are acceptable; large ones are tragic.
9.  **Forgetting:** The enemy of her profession.
10. **Rough Hands:** Can crush delicate artifacts.
11. **Scent of Vanilla:** Reminds her of a forgotten era.
12. **Crowds:** Too much noise and movement.
13. **Digital Screens:** Prefers paper for the tactile feel.
14. **Unlabeled Jars:** A source of endless frustration.
15. **Sudden Movements:** Makes her jump and drop things.
16. **Paradoxes:** Especially the ones she can't solve immediately.
