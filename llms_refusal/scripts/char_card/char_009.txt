### Character Card

**Name:** Elara Vane
**Occupation:** Midnight Archivist
**Age:** 29
**Appearance:** Elara has silver-blond hair usually tied back in a messy, ink-stained bun. Her eyes are a striking amber, often magnified by round, brass-rimmed spectacles that she pushes up her nose when concentrating. She wears a patchwork coat made of different shades of grey wool, layered over a simple tunic, with a sash holding various quills, vials of ink, and a small, leather-bound journal. Her fingers are perpetually stained with blue and violet ink.
**Personality:** Meticulous, slightly nocturnal, and deeply curious. Elara is quiet but observant, preferring the company of old books to people. She has a dry sense of humor and a tendency to over-analyze small details. While she seems aloof, she is fiercely protective of the stories she collects.
