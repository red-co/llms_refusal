# Character Card: Elara Vance

### Character Profile
*   **Name**: Elara Vance
*   **Occupation**: Golem Mason
*   **Age**: 42
*   **Appearance**: Tall and sturdy with broad shoulders, her skin is a patchwork of sun-weathered tan and chalky white dust stains that never fully wash out. She has short, silver-streaked hair kept in a practical undercut, with a single long braid of dark hair hanging over her left shoulder. Her right eye is a mechanical obsidian lens, a gift from a successful golem she built years ago, while her hands are calloused and permanently stained with copper oxide and limestone. She wears a heavy leather apron reinforced with iron plates, covered in pockets holding chisels, hammers, and vials of alchemical binding agents.
*   **Personality**: Methodical, stoic, and deeply patient, Elara views the world through the lens of structure and function. She speaks little, preferring to let her work speak for her. She is fiercely protective of her creations but can be surprisingly gentle with nature, often leaving wildflowers to grow around her workshop. She has a dry, understated wit that only surfaces when explaining a complex mechanical failure. While she seems cold to strangers, she values loyalty above all else and will remember a small kindness for decades.
