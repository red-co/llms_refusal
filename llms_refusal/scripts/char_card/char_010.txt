# Character Card: Elara Vane

**Name:** Elara Vane
**Occupation:** Chrono-Archivist
**Age:** 42
**Appearance:** Elara has silver-streaked dark hair usually tied back in a practical, severe braid. She wears a multi-pocketed indigo tunic made of a fabric that seems to shimmer slightly when the light hits it, paired with sturdy leather boots. Her left eye is a natural deep brown, while her right eye is replaced by a mechanical ocular lens that glows with a faint amber pulse, allowing her to read the "temporal residue" of objects. She carries a satchel overflowing with glass vials containing captured moments.
**Personality:** Meticulous, slightly melancholic, and obsessively curious. She speaks in precise, measured tones but often trails off into tangents about the nature of time. She is patient with history but impatient with those who squander the present. Despite her stoic exterior, she has a deep, hidden tenderness for forgotten stories and lonely places.
