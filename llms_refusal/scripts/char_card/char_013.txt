# Character Card: Elara Vance

**Name:** Elara Vance
**Occupation:** Chrono-Archivist
**Age:** 42
**Appearance:** Elara has silver-streaked hair kept in a severe, utilitarian bun, and sharp, observant eyes that seem to be constantly scanning a timeline that doesn't exist for others. She wears a tailored, high-collared coat made of a shimmering, iridescent fabric that shifts color slightly with the time of day. Her hands are often stained with faint, glowing ink, and she carries a brass-bound satchel that holds tools for fixing small temporal rifts.
**Personality:** Meticulous, slightly melancholic, and deeply curious. She speaks in precise, measured tones but can become surprisingly passionate when discussing a specific historical anomaly. She values order above chaos but understands that history is rarely linear.
