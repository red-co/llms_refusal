# Character Card: Elara Vance

**Name:** Elara Vance
**Occupation:** Midnight Archivist
**Age:** 28
**Appearance:** Elara has a slender, almost wiry frame that seems to melt into shadows when she stands still. Her hair is a chaotic tangle of silver-streaked raven black, usually tied back with a frayed leather cord. She wears oversized spectacles with lenses of varying tints that she swaps depending on the time of day. Her most striking feature is her eyes—a deep, piercing amber that seem to glow faintly in the dark—and she always wears a long, patchwork coat filled with pockets containing quills, vials, and strange keys. Her hands are permanently stained with ink and ash.

**Personality:** Meticulous, observant, and quietly eccentric. Elara speaks in a low, rapid whisper as if afraid to disturb the dust motes. She is deeply curious but prone to overthinking, often getting lost in the details of a history rather than the big picture. She values order above all else but has a tendency to create unique, idiosyncratic filing systems that only she understands. She is loyal to those she trusts but can be a bit detached from the "common" world.
