# Character Card: Elara Vance

**Name**: Elara Vance
**Occupation**: Urban Forager & Botanical Illustrator
**Age**: 29
**Appearance**: Elara has messy, shoulder-length chestnut hair often tucked behind a leather headband stained with charcoal and soil. She wears oversized, patched flannel shirts over a faded denim overall, paired with sturdy, mud-spattered boots. Her face is smudged with ink and dirt, her fingers permanently stained green from crushing herbs, and she has a distinctive small scar running through her left eyebrow from a childhood encounter with a thorny blackberry bush. Her eyes are a striking, piercing hazel that seem to constantly scan the horizon for hidden flora.
**Personality**: Meticulous, quietly optimistic, and fiercely protective of nature. Elara speaks softly but observes loudly, often getting lost in the intricate details of leaf veins or root structures. She is patient to a fault, capable of waiting hours for a specific bloom, but can become grumpy if rushed. She possesses a dry, subtle wit and prefers the company of moss and stray cats to crowds of people.
